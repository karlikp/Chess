[Instruction]

**White** 		**Black** 
6 = king		-6 = king
5 = queen		-5 = queen
4 = rook		-4 = rook
3 = bishop		-3 = bishop
2 = knight		-2 = knight
1 = pawn		-1 = pawn

Move making is possible by choice position of piece, which you're going to move.
e.g. D2

Next, need to click 'enter button' on keyboard.

The program show you scope available position for chosen piece.
e.g. [D3][D4]

Then you can choose the position where your piece will travel.
e.g. D4  

Next, need to click 'enter button' on keyboard again.
